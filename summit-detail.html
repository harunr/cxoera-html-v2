Focus Areas

Key Themes & focus-areas on this summit.

Register Now
Keynote Talk

Gives attending delegates the opportunity to hear from leading voices in the industry. These talks feature relevant topics, key issues and business opportunities aligned with the speaker’s experience and expertise, selected by the speaker in concert with the summit’s Executive Committee.

Thought Leadership

Led by an executive from the solution provider, Thought Leadership sessions provide comprehensive overviews of current business concerns, offering strategies and solutions for success. This is a unique opportunity to access the perspective of a leading member of the solution provider community.

Executive Visions

Sessions are panel discussions that enable in-depth ŉ exchanges on critical business topics. Led by a moderator, these sessions encourage attending delegates to address industry challenges and gain valuable insight through interaction with expert panel members.

Round Table

Led by an industry analyst, expert or a member of the vendor community, Roundtables are open-forum sessions with strategic guidance. Attending delegates gather to collaborate on common issues and challenges within a format that allows them to get things done.

Executive Exhange

Offer one-on-one interaction between executives and solution providers. This is an opportunity for both parties to make key business contacts, ask direct questions and get the answers they need. Session content is prearranged and based on mutual interest.

Focus Group

Led by an executive from the solution provider, Thought Leadership sessions provide comprehensive overviews of current business concerns, offering strategies and solutions for success. This is a unique opportunity to access the perspective of a leading member of the solution provider community.

Networking Session

Take various unique forms, merging enjoyable, relaxing and productive activities with an environment conducive to in-depth conversation. These gatherings allow attendees to wind down between sessions and one-on-one interactions, while still furthering discussions and being productive.
